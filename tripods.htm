Cardellini Tri-Hat

The most versatile 3-legged high hat ever. Easy leveling. Interchangeable legs slide up/down and angle at the ends of three radiating arms. Arms can be pivoted or slid in and out to control where feet land. Comes with 6" and 12" legs with rubber feet. Maximum height of Mitchell mount is 15". Minimum height is 3" (in low mode). Components are 1¼" speedrail so custom adaptations, such as condor rigs, are easily accomplished using 1¼" pipe. Mitchell plate and pipes are hard black anodized. Works great in back seat of car, on steps, or on steep or rocky ground.
