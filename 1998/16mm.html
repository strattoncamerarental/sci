  Using 35mm format lenses on 16mm cameras.....

Lenses are generally intended for use in either the 35mm or 16mm format and have mounts appropriate to their optical coverage. Because the 16SR3 and 16SR2ePL have the same lens mount intended for 35mm cameras, they can accept any lens in this catalog. This allows a greater selection of lenses for 16mm cinematography. There are a few limitations for the Super 16 format, so inquire about lens coverage and compatibility for this format.
