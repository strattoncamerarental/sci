The CINETAPE MEASURE system

A small, rugged, lightweight and quiet ultrasonic range finder. It is compatible with all lenses and cameras. The system is easy to set up and use, making it the first choice of professional filmmakers. | Download manual

CINETAPE 2 Control

This is lighter and smaller than the original CINETAPE. It continuously calculates the distance between the camera’s film plane and the subject. All of the original CINETAPE features are included in the CINETAPE 2. In addition a wireless radio was added that communicates with the AIR MOBILE 2 at a 500 foot range. BlueTooth compatibility was also added to connect the CINETAPE 2 to an iPhone via the CINETAPE AIR App that adds many additional features to the new CINETAPE 2. | Download manual

The innovative Cinematography Electronics SENSOR ASSEMBLY consists of two small cone-shaped horns, a miniature pan tilt mechanism, a mounting stud and an electrical connector. The horns send and receive ultrasonic signals that are used to calculate the distance measurement. Mounting the SENSOR ASSEMBLY onto a camera package is simple with the combo stud. With its pan and tilt adjust/lock knobs, aiming the horns towards the subject is quick and straightforward. The SENSOR ASSEMBLY interfaces to the control via a short detachable Sensor Cable. | Download manual
