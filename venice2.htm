Full-Frame full-width 36‑mm 8K

In full-frame, you can use the full width of the sensor (8640 pixels) for widescreen spherical 2.39:1 or Large Format Scope. Full-Frame can be used creatively in a number of ways—for example, to allow for extra shallow depth of ﬁeld or super-wide shooting.

Second Generation Enhancements

Developed alongside VENICE 2, the VENICE Extension System 2 offers a wider bandwidth to support the VENICE 2 with an 8K sensor, over twice as much cable distance (up to 12 m) without a repeater box, additional control buttons and a built-in gyro sensor. It once again expands the realms of possibility for cinematography.
