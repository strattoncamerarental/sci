  • WCU-3

The Wireless Compact Unit WCU-3 handset has the capability to control two axes, plus camera run/stop. With a built-in programmable focus knob and thumb slider, one battery can power the unit for up to 12 hours of continuous operation.

The Zoom Main Unit ZMU-3A is a handgrip zoom controller that can be mounted to the WCU-3 to create a three axis wireless controller or can be detached from the WCU-3 and mounted to a pan handle to use as a wired zoom controller. It includes a force sensitive zoom knob that assistants’ thumbs all over the world are familiar with.
