ALEXA 35 is a 4K Super 35 camera that elevates digital cinematography to unprecedented heights. ARRI's first new sensor in 12 years builds on the evolution of the ALEXA family, delivering 2.5 stops more dynamic range, film-like highlight handling, better low light performance, and richer colors.

Shooting in Low Light with ALEXA 35

ARRI's quick guide provides recommendations for achieving the best low light performance when shooting with the ARRI ALEXA 35 and discusses Enhanced Sensitivity and ARRI Textures in detail. Download Low Light Quick Guide

Continuous Power

Using the bebob 90wH hot swap battery, on-board power can be continuously supplied by the regular 155wH B-mount batteries.
