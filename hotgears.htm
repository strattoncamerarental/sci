Hot Gears™ is an award-winning 2- and 3-axis microprocessor remote camera controller that can be easily attached to our Arrihead.

It is designed to allow the Cinematographer and the Camera Operator to utilize the Arrihead as a very cost effective remote system.

Capable of real time record/playback

Hot Gears™ will self-calibrate and continuously match the exact position of the wheels and the motors to yield a repeatable position as the operator moves the wheels. It automatically ensures that the camera comes to a safe/soft stop before the geared head runs into its mechanical end.
