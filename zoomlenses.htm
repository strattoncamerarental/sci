Information about Cooke Varotal/Cinetal image circles

New life for classic zooms

The Veydra 1.2x expander makes it possible to use zoom lenses from the 35mm Academy format era with current generation Super 35 and larger sensor cameras.
The expander works by enlarging the image circle of vintage zoom lenses from 28mm ➤ 36mm while introducing only a ½ stop of light loss during the process.
  • Original ➤ Converted focal length:
  • Angenieux HR 25-250mm ➤ (30-300mm T4.2)
  • Optimo 24-290mm ➤ (29-348mm T3.4)
  • Cooke 18-100mm ➤ (21.6-120mm T3.6)
  • Cooke 20-100mm ➤ (24-120mm T3.7)
  • Cooke 25-250mm MK3 ➤ (30-300mm T4.5)
