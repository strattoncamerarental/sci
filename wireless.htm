1303 & 703 BOLT

SmallHD and Teradek have joined forces to create a wireless monitoring system that’s easy to see and power. Easily pair the 1303 or 703 BOLT with Teradek BOLT 500, 1000, or 3000 transmitters. Equipped with a built-in wireless receiver and software tools like real-time 3D LUTs, the 1303 and 703 BOLT are the ultimate all in one solution (no cage required).

Standard Definition video

The Canatrans XMTR features variable RF power levels, variable video and audio gain, video absent notification and wide range input voltage. When paired with the Transvideo Pegasus receiver and Rainbow II monitor, you have a very robust system that has been the standard SD video assist system for film cameras for many years.
